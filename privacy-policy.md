# Privacy Policy for VLR Pick Bans Scraper

**Last Updated: November 19, 2025**

## Introduction

VLR Pick Bans Scraper ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data from VLR.gg

Our extension collects publicly available data from VLR.gg, including:

- Map pick and ban information from professional Valorant matches
- Team names and match results
- Tournament and event information
- Match dates and map selections

### Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device:

- User preferences and settings
- Cached match data for offline access
- Analysis results and statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

- Display map pick/ban statistics and trends
- Provide matchup analysis and insights
- Improve user experience through cached data
- Store user preferences for the extension

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

- We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties
- We **DO NOT** collect personal identification information
- We **DO NOT** track your browsing history outside of VLR.gg
- All data processing happens locally on your device

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the extension

## Permissions Explanation

Our extension requires the following permissions:

- **storage**: To save your preferences and cache match data locally
- **activeTab**: To access VLR.gg pages and extract match information
- **Host permission (vlr.gg)**: To scrape publicly available match data from VLR.gg

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data stored by the extension
- Delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension
- Opt-out by not using the extension

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

## Compliance

This extension complies with:

- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- Google's User Data Policy
- GDPR principles (where applicable)
